🐕 The meaning of the name

OLO-G holds two minds. ‘oroji’ (오로지) — to focus wholeheartedly on what matters to us. ‘-ology’ — to dig in relentlessly toward true mastery. And nudge the o and l a little closer… dog 🐶. Friendly, but sincere — that's our kind of wit.

🧭 Mission

We're going to make everyone in the world a little happier.Let's Make the World a Little Happier!

First, we have to be happy. Then our players, and finally the world. Happy makers pass happiness to players, and happy players pass it on to someone beside them — and so the world grows a little happier.
