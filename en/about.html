The OLO-G Story

People who work like it's a hobby, with happiness as the top priority. Here's how we're making the world a little happier.

🐕 The meaning of the name

The name OLO-G holds two minds.

‘oroji’ (오로지) — to focus wholeheartedly on what matters to us.
‘-ology’ — to study and dig in relentlessly toward true mastery.

olog… nudge the o and l a little closer and doesn't it look like dog? 🐶 Friendly but sincere — that's our kind of wit.

🧭 Mission

We're going to make everyone in the world a little happier. Let's Make the World a Little Happier!

First, we have to be happy. No overtime, earning well, fun work, good people. Everyone's conditions for happiness differ, but we all share one thing: if I'm not happy right now, none of this means anything.

Next, our players have to be happy. We think from the perspective of every kind of player, building gifts that bring fun, wonder and joy.

Finally, the world has to be happy. Happy makers pass happiness to players, and happy players pass it on to those around them. That's how the world grows, even a little, happier.

🔍 Vision

Games add ‘A Spoonful of Happiness’ to our lives. Games are a way to add focus and joy to everything in everyday life.

It was only natural that OLO-G started with games — there's no better starting point for adding fun and happiness to everyone's lives. But what about people who dislike games? We believe no one truly dislikes games — they just haven't met the right one for them yet.

OLO-G's stage isn't limited to games. Comics, music, culture — anything that makes people happy. Our goal isn't the grand one of inventing what never existed; rather, we'll seek out beautiful values we've forgotten and share them in our own way.

⚖️ Values

We love people and adventure, and we dream of ideals — then make them real through grit and stubbornness.

Warmth

🎁 Gifts

We make special gifts for the people we love. The delighted look on their face, a happy memory that lasts. We agonize endlessly, imagining that moment of giving.

🐶 Friends

Anyone who works with us, even briefly, stays a friend. We keep good connections alive and grow into the best of colleagues.

Adventure

🗺️ Frontier

Every time, we dive into a new audience and a new genre. No boundaries — because everyone deserves the chance to meet a game they'll enjoy.

⚔️ Courage

We gladly embrace the fear of heading a different way from everyone else. If we're not different, there's no reason to exist. We write our own formula for success.

Ideals

🏖️ Freedom

If freedom brings happiness, we work as freely as it takes. We decide together who joins, how much each contributes, and where and when we work. It's the privilege and the edge of a new-generation team.

🙆 Equality

We pursue an equal, discrimination-free, deeply inclusive atmosphere. Roles and responsibilities may differ, but every member is respected exactly the same.

Stubbornness

🔍 Quality

We always insist on the best effort — not the ‘greatest’, but the ‘utmost’. We compete on originality, never miss the details that move people, and review ourselves demandingly.

♟️ Structure

We work freely, but never in chaos. We plan thoroughly and build structure. We don't waste our limited chances and time.

🐾 ologgies — a forever membership

At the heart of OLO-G is membership. Anyone who works with us, even briefly, stays a forever member of OLO-G — an ‘ologgie’, no matter how long they were here. We take pride in every shared moment, and even after parting ways we cheer on each other's growth and news.
